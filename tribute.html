Tribute to Mahatma Gandhi

-> Mahatma Gandhi, recognized as the "Father of the Nation," emerged as a key figure in India's fight for independence against British colonial rule.
-> His approach centered around nonviolent resistance, famously termed as Satyagraha, which became a pivotal force in the nation's quest for freedom.
-> Gandhi's philosophy emphasized the power of truth and peaceful protest, guiding India through a transformative journey towards self-determination.
-> His leadership and commitment to nonviolence left an enduring impact on the course of history.


"Let us remember and honor the life and legacy of Mahatma Gandhi, a true symbol of peace and humanity."
